About Tomás Ó Dálaigh

Tomás Ó Dálaigh is a 28-year-old virtuoso fiddle player from Waterford, Ireland, now based in Cork. Known for his contemporary take on traditional Irish music, Tomás's performances are a vibrant mix of old and new, captivating audiences worldwide.

Growing up in a family steeped in musical tradition, Tomás picked up the fiddle at a young age. His passion for the instrument and dedication to his craft have led him to perform across Europe, the US, and China. With each performance, he brings a fresh perspective to the rich tapestry of Irish music.

Tomás has released several albums, each reflecting his unique style that blends tradition with innovation. His music not only preserves the essence of Irish culture but also pushes its boundaries, making it accessible and exciting for new audiences.

In addition to his solo career, Tomás collaborates with other musicians, exploring new genres and expanding his musical horizons. His work is a testament to the versatility and timelessness of the fiddle, and his journey continues to inspire many.
