About Tomás

Tomás Ó Dálaigh is a 28-year-old virtuoso fiddle player hailing from Waterford, Ireland, and currently based in Cork. With a deep-rooted passion for traditional Irish music, Tomás has modernized the fiddle's sound, infusing it with contemporary flair while staying true to his heritage. His journey has seen him perform across Europe, the US, and China, captivating audiences with his dynamic performances and heartfelt melodies. Tomás's music is a testament to the timeless appeal of Irish culture, seamlessly blending the old with the new.
