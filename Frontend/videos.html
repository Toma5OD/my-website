Videos

Unending Day is a peaceful and calming track, composed during the lockdown in Ireland. This piece captures cherished memories of rare sunny days and evokes a sense of tranquility and nostalgia. The serene melodies are designed to soothe and uplift, offering a moment of respite in challenging times.

The One That Was Lost Set is a memorable performance with the Waterford band Cuisle, captured in China. This set highlights the collaborative spirit and cultural exchange that defines my musical journey, bringing Irish music to audiences around the world.

Tune Set by Liz Carroll performed by my band Over the Bar in Bologna. This performance showcases our dynamic interpretation of Liz Carroll's compositions, blending traditional melodies with our unique style for an engaging live experience.

The Beauty Queen of Leenane is a piece I composed with Patrick Clinton for the play by Martin McDonagh. This track, recorded during my time at UCC, features the contributions of talented musicians and dear friends, capturing the essence of collaboration and creativity.

M3 is a creative project born out of the COVID-19 pandemic, featuring multiple fiddle lines and synchronized audio and video performances.
