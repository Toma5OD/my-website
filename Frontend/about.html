About Tomás Ó Dálaigh

Tomás Ó Dálaigh is a modern Irish fiddle player celebrated for his dynamic performances and deep connection to Irish music traditions. He has toured extensively across Europe, the USA, and beyond, captivating audiences with his vibrant sound.

A graduate of University College Cork, Tomás combines his academic background in music and Irish with years of professional experience, offering a unique and authentic musical journey.
