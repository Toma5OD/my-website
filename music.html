N95 is a dynamic track created during the COVID-19 pandemic, featuring a fusion of modern folk, electronic music, and traditional Irish influences. Collaborators on this project include Conor Broderick (co-producer), Alex Borwick (co-producer, mixing mastering, trombone), Richie Delahunty (guitars), Dale McKay (hangpan), Cein Daly (bass), Aine Ni Dhalaigh (vocals), and Fiachra Kinder on drums. This track reflects a blend of energetic electro-acoustic vibes, inspired by artists like Tame Impala, and showcases the versatility and innovative spirit of contemporary Irish music.

Rún is an introspective composition I crafted over a decade ago. This piece delves deep into themes of loneliness and the quest for meaning in an often perplexing world. The track combines traditional Irish elements with a modern twist, creating a hauntingly beautiful soundscape that resonates with listeners on a profound level.

Myself and Dean Griffen playing some tunes from all over. Recorded by Matt Griffin in his studio in Dingle.
